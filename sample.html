Dental Implants

Dental implants are a permanent solution for missing teeth, offering durability and a natural appearance. They restore your ability to chew and speak comfortably while improving overall oral health. Made from biocompatible materials, implants fuse with the jawbone for long-lasting support.

3-Step Treatment Process

  • Step 1: The dentist places a titanium implant into the jawbone.
  • Step 2: The bone heals and fuses with the implant over a few months.
  • Step 3: A custom-made crown is attached for a natural-looking tooth.
